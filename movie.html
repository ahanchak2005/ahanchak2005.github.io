I like to read novels, mainly fiction.

Some of my favorite writers are:

Rabindranath Tagore, Fyodor Dostoevsky, Sarat Chandra Chattopadhyay, Bankim Chandra Chattopadhyay, Bibhutibhushan Bandyopadhyay, Shankar, Khaled Hosseini, Friedrich Nietzsche, Haruki Murakami, and Dan Brown.

I also enjoy reading the humorous mythological series by Nikhad Bangali (Sampad Barik) very much.
